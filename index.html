About Me

Hi, I’m Sarah, a recent graduate of the Software Developer program at Manitoba Institute of Trades and Technolgoy, with a strong foundation in full-stack development. Through hands-on projects, I’ve gained experience with tools like JavaScript, React, .NET, and relational databases. I also completed a seven-week practicum, honing my problem-solving and collaboration skills. I’m passionate about building innovative solutions and ready to contribute as a Junior Full-Stack Developer.


Before entering the Software Developer program, I worked as an Educational Assistant for 10 years and loved helping students learn and grow. These experiences taught me patience, adaptability, and the importance of clear communication—skills I bring to every project.


My interest in software development started in high school, where I discovered my love for computer science. I had always enjoyed playing video games, and was interested in technology growing up. I took computer science courses in university and loved them, but life took me down a different path. The desire to learn coding never left me, so I enrolled in the Software Development program at MITT. Now, I’m excited to build impactful solutions and keep learning in the tech world.
